Yin Yoga

Yin Yoga is a practice of long held poses. Gently come into a pose until you find an edge that feels appropriate for your body, and hold there. Place your awareness on your body and breath. If you then feel your body is opening up, feel free to move into a fuller expression of the pose. Yoga is never painful. Listen to your body and do what feels right for you.
